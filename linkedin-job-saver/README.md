LinkedIn Job Downloader helps you save job descriptions from LinkedIn with a single click. Stop copying and pasting or taking screenshots - this extension automatically captures all the important details and saves them as a clean, well-formatted HTML file.

**Key Features**

* One-Click Download - A convenient download button appears directly on LinkedIn job pages
* Complete Information - Captures job title, company, location, salary, work type, posting date, and applicant count
* Beautifully Formatted - Saves as a clean HTML file that preserves the original formatting and looks great when printed
* Organized Storage - Files are named using company and position for easy sorting and finding
* Works Offline - Access saved job listings anytime without an internet connection
* Privacy Focused - Your data stays local; nothing is uploaded or shared

**Perfect For**

* Job seekers tracking multiple applications
* Recruiters saving position details
* HR professionals archiving job postings
* Comparing job opportunities side by side
* Creating a personal jobs database

**How It Works**

* Visit any LinkedIn job listing
* Click the download button that appears in the bottom right corner
* The job description is automatically saved to your downloads folder
* Open the HTML file in any browser to view or print

Privacy & Permissions
This extension only activates on LinkedIn job pages and requires minimal permissions:

* Access to LinkedIn job pages only
* Download capability to save files
* No data is collected, shared, or uploaded

Simple, fast, and privacy-friendly - LinkedIn Job Downloader helps you keep track of job opportunities without the hassle of manual copying.
